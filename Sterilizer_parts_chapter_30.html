1. The Chamber

The central, hermetically sealed pressure vessel where device payloads are placed for sterilization exposure. It is constructed exclusively of heavy-gauge, corrosion-resistant stainless steel to prevent long-term fatigue or cracking from deep stress cycles.

MDRT Quality Catch: Overloading sets or nesting items incorrectly chokes steam circulation boundaries, leading to cold spots and micro-condensation trapping.
